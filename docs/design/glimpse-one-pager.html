GLIMPSE

The trust layer for direct giving on Solana

Investor Brief
February 2026
Pre-Seed

Investment Thesis

Glimpse turns one-time donors into repeat donors through proof.

Charitable giving is massive, but donor behavior is fragile. Glimpse closes the missing trust loop by pairing instant wallet-to-wallet donations with post-gift proof of impact.

Core Insight

In giving, the second donation is the activation event. Products that increase second-gift conversion can outperform without needing larger donor acquisition budgets.

$592.5B U.S. charitable giving (2024)
$1B+ Crypto donations (2024 record)
73.7% First-time donors do not return
0% Glimpse platform fee (today)

Problem

Giving is high volume but low visibility.

  • Most donors never receive concrete evidence of what changed after giving.
  • Nonprofit reporting cycles are delayed and disconnected from gift events.
  • Trust decays when outcomes are unclear, even if intent is strong.
  • Acquisition spend rises when retention remains structurally low.

Product Strategy

Make every donation observable and repeatable.

  • Direct campaign giving through Solana wallets.
  • On-chain receipts provide immutable transaction evidence.
  • Updates ("glimpses") show what happened after funds land.
  • A vault model supports one-time and recurring donor behavior.

Behavior Loop

Proof converts intent into habit.

1. Commit

Choose a campaign and donation cadence.

2. Settle

Transfer executes instantly on Solana rails.

3. Verify

Receipt is visible on-chain with clear attribution.

4. Return

Impact updates trigger repeat gifts and streak behavior.

Retention Wedge

The second gift compounds platform value.

Existing donor data supports the thesis: retention improves sharply as giving count increases. Glimpse is designed to move users from one gift to many.

  • 1 gift: 13.9% donor retention
  • 2 gifts: 59.0% donor retention
  • 7 gifts: 86.2% donor retention
