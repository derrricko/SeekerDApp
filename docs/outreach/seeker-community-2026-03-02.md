## Engagement Strategy

### For Tier 1 (Seeker natives):
1. **Reply to their existing Seeker-related tweets** — "I'm building a donation dApp on Seeker called Glimpse. Saw you're deep in the ecosystem — would love your take on it."
2. **Share a screenshot or demo** — these people already care about Seeker dApps and want to see what's being built.
3. **Ask for feedback** — "Would love your take on this" works especially well with beta testers like @HumphreyFirst.

### For Tier 2 (Solana community):
1. **Like and reply to something they said first** — build familiarity before mentioning your app.
2. **Lead with the story, not the product** — "I'm a solo founder building a zero-fee donation dApp on Seeker. My son is one year old and I'm trying to build the world I want him to grow up in."
3. **Connect on shared values** — parents (@CaliBTC_, @amksy_, @btcbob, @MacOnChain) will resonate with the mission.

### Tactical notes:
- Batch your engagement: 10-15 genuine interactions per session, not 100 spam replies
- Track who responds — those are your early adopters
- Re-run this search weekly as the community grows fast
