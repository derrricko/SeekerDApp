04 — Voice & Tone
Bold. Mission-driven.
Glimpse speaks with confidence, not arrogance. With purpose, not preachiness. Every word should feel like it belongs on a protest sign or a journal entry — never a corporate press release.
We Are
Direct
Short sentences. Real numbers. No hedging. Lead with the action, not the feeling.
We Are
Purposeful
Every word earns its place. We say what matters and nothing more. Copy is a design element.
We Are
Human
We talk like people, not platforms. Names, not "users." Stories, not "use cases."
We Are
Confident
We believe in what we're building. No apologies, no qualifiers, no "we hope to."
We Are Not
Preachy
We don't guilt-trip. We inspire action through proof, not emotion.
Hypey
No "revolutionary" or "game-changing." The work speaks for itself.
Do / Don't
Do
"$25. A warm shower. Fresh clothes. You'll see the proof."
Don't
"Your generous donation can help make a real difference in someone's life today!"
Do
"Every transaction on Solana. Transparent. Permanent."
Don't
"Leveraging cutting-edge blockchain technology to revolutionize charitable giving!"
Do
"James got a warm meal today. Here's the receipt."
Don't
"Thanks to our amazing community, we were able to facilitate a positive outcome!"
