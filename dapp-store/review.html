Short Description

Zero fees. Real impact. Every dollar on-chain.

Long Description

Most people's entire lives will be forever changed by a few thousand dollars. Glimpse makes sure every one of those dollars actually arrives.


Glimpse is a USDC donation app where 100% of what you give goes directly to people in need. No platform fees. No processing cuts. Every transaction is recorded on the Solana blockchain so you can verify it yourself.


When you donate, you can describe who you want to help and leave a personal note. Glimpse uses that context to match your donation to a specific person or cause. A message thread opens immediately so you can communicate directly and follow the impact of your gift.


How it works:

  1. Connect your Seeker wallet
  2. Pick a campaign and enter any USDC amount
  3. Add match context and a note for the recipient
  4. Review the details and sign
  5. Follow real updates in your message thread

Zero fees. Zero middlemen. Built on Solana for the Seeker community.

Testing Instructions (for reviewers)

  1. Open the app and tap the Give button.
  2. Connect your Seeker wallet when prompted.
  3. Enter any USDC amount and select a campaign.
  4. Optionally add match context and a note.
  5. Tap Review Donation, then Confirm and Sign in your wallet.
  6. Verify the donation appears in the Glimpses tab with CONFIRMED status.
  7. Verify a message thread appears in the Messages tab.
